Privacy Policy for Untiled: A Puzzle Game

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

Untiled: A Puzzle Game ("the App") is a puzzle game developed by Evan Czako. This privacy policy explains how the App handles information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. No data is sent to any server or third party.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • High score — your best score, saved so it persists between sessions

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future update, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
